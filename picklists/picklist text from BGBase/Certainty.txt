Certainty

1	long shot - a poor match in the gazetteer or map and/or unresolved ambiguity
2	possible - a reasonable match in the gazetteer or map and/or a significant ambiguity
3	probable - close match for the locality name in the gazetteer or map, with possible, although unlikely, ambiguity
4  	certain - an exact match for the locality name in the gazetteer or map; or information derived from a GPS
